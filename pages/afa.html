America For Animals

Advocating animal rights through technology

America for Animals is a registered 501(c)3 nonprofit organization. It aspires to give everyone the potential to help animals by creating apps to help advocates to connect and communities to organize. Being very passionate about preserving nature and supporting animal rights myself, I was very excited to join this organization as a web developer.

The America For Animals website was designed and developed on wordpress, a content management resource. As with my other sites I went through the steps of requirement gathering, designing, prototyping and development. I had to customize the 'Accesspress' theme heavily to give my client the minimalisic and AFA branded look they desired. Also I familiarized myself with wiring with paypal and salesforce backends for customer form entries from the front-end. Finally this website went through rigorous testing on multiple devices before being deployed.

Overall this was a very satisfying and enriching technical experience and I am constantly trying to improve it by adding SEO principles and reviewing the Google Analytics for this site.
