About Me

I am an aspiring visual designer based out of Sandiego, CA. Creating designs that are meaningful, practical and at the same time beautiful is my passion. In my past life I used to be a software engineer. After 10 years in the technology industry, I let it go to quench my creative thirst by pursuing interactive media design. Speaking through digital mediums provides a balance I seek between technology and design.
