About Me

I am an interaction/web designer based out of sunny Sandiego, CA. After working as a software engineer for 10 years in the telecom industry, I decided to pursue my real passion for design. I strongly beleive that every design in this day and age should combine A (arts), B (bits/binary) and C (culture) to be fully successful. Also, user experience is a big part of everyday life, it is NOT an optional process anymore, so no product design can be complete without embracing it at every stage. Speaking through a digital medium provides a balance I seek between technology and creativity. Want to know more about my skills? Feel free to download my resume here, or send me a message through the contact form. You can also read about my design projects in my blog.
